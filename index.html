Ghana

Ghana is a country located along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean, in West Africa. Spanning a landmass of 238,535 km2 (92,098 sq mi), Ghana is bordered by the Ivory Coast in the west, Burkina Faso in the north, Togo in the east, and the Atlantic Ocean in the south. Ghana means "Warrior King" in the Soninke language. It earned this name from the medieval Ghana Empire. The population of Ghana is approximately 34 million, making it the 48th most populous country in the world. The capital and largest city is Accra; other cities are Kumasi, Tamale, and Sekondi-Takoradi.
